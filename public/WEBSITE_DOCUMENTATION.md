**Our Story**
Ghar Ka Khana (घर का खाना – "Home Food") was born from a simple yet powerful observation: millions of people living away from home deeply miss the taste of authentic, home-cooked meals. At the same time, countless talented home cooks possess the skills and passion to prepare delicious food but lack the platform to share it with others. We bridge this gap. Founded in 2024, Ghar Ka Khana is a revolutionary platform that connects hungry customers with verified home chefs in their neighborhood. We believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious, freshly-prepared, home-style meals—not just those who can cook! Our platform empowers home cooks to turn their culinary skills into a thriving business while providing customers with an authentic alternative to restaurant food. Every meal on our platform is prepared with the same love and care as if it were made in your own kitchen.

**Mission & Vision**
*   **Mission:** To revolutionize the food delivery industry by providing access to authentic, healthy, and affordable home-cooked meals while empowering local home chefs to earn a sustainable income from their culinary talents.
*   **Vision:** To become India's most trusted platform for homemade food, creating a nationwide network of home kitchens that serves millions of customers with the taste of home, no matter where they are.

**What Makes Us Different**
*   **Authentic Recipes:** Traditional family recipes passed down through generations.
*   **Fresher Food:** Meals prepared in smaller batches with fresh ingredients.
*   **Healthier Options:** Less oil, no preservatives, and balanced nutrition.
*   **Affordable Pricing:** Lower overhead means better prices for you.
*   **Local Connection:** Support neighborhood chefs and build community.

**Our Values**
*   **Quality & Hygiene:** Every home chef is verified for kitchen hygiene, food handling practices, and quality standards.

*   **Authentic Cooking:** We celebrate the diversity of Indian home cooking.
